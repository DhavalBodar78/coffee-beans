A Journey of Coffee Excellence

Founded in 2018, CoffeeBean began as a small dream in the heart of our founder's kitchen. What started as a passion for perfect coffee has grown into a beloved community gathering place where every cup tells a story.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment where friends could gather, ideas could flourish, and memories could be made over the perfect cup of coffee.

Today, we continue to honor that mission by sourcing the finest beans from around the world, training passionate baristas, and creating a space where every visit feels like coming home.
